About Luxe Furniture

Crafting beautiful spaces since 1995

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Luxe Furniture began as a small family-owned workshop in Brooklyn, New York. Our passion for exceptional craftsmanship and timeless design has driven us to become one of the most trusted names in premium home furnishings.

For nearly three decades, we've been committed to creating furniture that doesn't just fill a space, but transforms it. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected or custom-designed to meet the highest standards of quality, comfort, and aesthetic appeal.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across the country, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every piece of furniture that bears our name is a testament to the craftsmanship, attention to detail, and dedication to customer satisfaction that has defined us from the beginning.
