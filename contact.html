Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging materials.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options with approved credit. Zero-interest plans are available for purchases over $1,000.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2-4 weeks. Express delivery options are available for select items. Custom furniture typically takes 6-8 weeks.

Can I schedule a showroom visit?

Absolutely! Walk-ins are welcome during business hours, or you can schedule a private consultation with one of our design experts.
