Custom Furniture Design

Can't find exactly what you're looking for? Our master craftsmen can create bespoke furniture pieces tailored to your exact specifications, ensuring a perfect fit for your space and style.

White Glove Delivery & Assembly

Enjoy hassle-free delivery with our premium white glove service. Our professional team handles everything from transportation to assembly, ensuring your furniture arrives safely and is set up perfectly.

  • Expert assembly and placement

  • Final quality check and walkthrough

Breathe new life into your cherished furniture pieces. Our restoration experts can repair, refinish, and restore antique and modern furniture to its former glory.
